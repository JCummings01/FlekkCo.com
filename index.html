Upgrade your Fitbit Flex.

We created Flekk to solve a problem. That problem was the lack of options when it came to changing the stock silicone rubber wristband of the Fitbit Flex.

The Fitbit Flex is a great piece of technology, but it's form factor leaves something to be desired. Looking like a bloated Livestrong bracelet, it doesn't do much to fit in and complement your wardrobe or personal sense of style. It also doesn't stay clasped and is prone to falling off. Lastly, it's been widely known to react negatively with some wearer's skin and cause rashes.

Custom colors and bands are available to help you make yours truly unique and your own. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.
